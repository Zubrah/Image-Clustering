    Product Details

    An aromatic-woody scent with a captivating trail that pays homage to masculine liberation. A classic perfume held in a dark and mysterious blue bottle.
    BLEU DE CHANEL Parfum is a well-crafted fragrance with a pure, deep character. A trademark that is profoundly masculine and exudes self-assurance.

    BLEU DE CHANEL Parfum is a fragrant, woody scent with a strong aroma. It begins with a powerful freshness and ends with a precious accord of New Caledonian sandalwood, which unfolds its generous, powerful notes in a dense and sophisticated trail.

    Top notes: Lemon Zest, Bergamot, Mint and Artemisia

    Middle notes: Lavender, Geranium, Pineapple and Green Notes

    Base notes; Sandalwood, Cedar, Amberwood, Iso E Super and Tonka Bean
